Support
Computers

Since 2019, we have been a member and partner of the international Microsoft IoT program, within which we support the company's products:

Microsoft Inc.

We provide technical support:

  • Microsoft® Office 365
  • Microsoft® Office 2019
  • Microsoft® Office 2019 (for macOS)
  • Microsoft® Office 2016
  • Microsoft® Office 2016 (for macOS)

In addition to installing and supporting major editions of Office with the standard suite of applications, we also provide an advanced package that includes Microsoft Query for viewing and filtering information from databases.

In addition to retail sales, Microsoft offers the HUP Home Use Program, which gives members of a participating organization access to home use of Microsoft Office products.
